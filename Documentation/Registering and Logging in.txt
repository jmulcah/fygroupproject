How to Register: 

1. Click Register button and you will be redirected to localhost:5000/register
2. Enter all your details such as email, name etc.
3. You will automatically be set as not an admin.
4. Click Register and your details will be added to the database. 


How to login:
*You must have registered in order to login*
1. Enter the email and password associated with your registered account. 
2. Click login.
3. You will then be brought to either the user dashboard or the admin dashboard depending on your admin status.
